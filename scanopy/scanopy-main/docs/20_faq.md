# Frequently Asked Questions

### What are the minimum system requirements?

For the server, we recommend at least 2GB of RAM and 2 CPU cores. For the daemon, 512MB of RAM is sufficient.

### Can Scanopy scan multiple VLANs?

Yes. You will need to deploy a daemon in each VLAN you want to scan. See the [Multi-VLAN Deployment](/docs/daemons/multi-vlan/) guide for details.

### Can Scanopy scan WiFi networks?

Yes, as long as the daemon is connected to the WiFi network, it can scan it like any other network.

### Does Scanopy support IPv6?

Not at this time. IPv6 support is on our roadmap.

### How is Scanopy licensed?

The self-hosted version of Scanopy is licensed under the AGPL-3.0. We also offer a [commercial license](/docs/getting-started/introduction/#licensing) for organizations that cannot comply with the AGPL-3.0 terms. Scanopy Cloud is a subscription-based service.

### Do you offer a free trial?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for Scanopy Cloud. The self-hosted version is free to use under the AGPL-3.0 license.

### How can I request a new feature?

You can open a [feature request on GitHub](https://github.com/scanopy/scanopy/issues/new?template=feature_request.md).
